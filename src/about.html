About This Project

GSAU.gg is an official Gelsoft AU Discord community project that helps Australian gel blaster enthusiasts find the best prices across multiple online retailers.

Community

This project is maintained by the Gelsoft AU Discord community and is available for everyone to use. Join us to chat with fellow gel blaster enthusiasts:

Join Gelsoft AU Discord

What We Do

We automatically collect publicly available product and pricing information from Australian gel blaster retailers and present it in an easy-to-compare format. This helps you make informed purchasing decisions without having to manually check multiple websites.

Data Sources

All product information displayed on this site is sourced from publicly accessible APIs and web pages provided by the retailers themselves. We do not have any special access or partnerships with these stores.

Privacy & Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use this site and which stores are most popular.

If you'd prefer not to be tracked, any ad blocker (like uBlock Origin) will block Google Analytics automatically. The site works perfectly fine without it.

Your theme preference is stored locally in your browser and never leaves your device.
